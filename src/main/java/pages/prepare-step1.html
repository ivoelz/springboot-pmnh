1. Prepare Before Steaming

First you'll want to purge your steam wand. This means turning on your steam wand and letting it remove water and any build up in the wand. This makes a big difference in the consistency of the milk.

Now pour your milk into your steam pitcher up to how ever many ounces you need. Make sure your milk is cold, cold milk is better to steam with because it gives you time to create the perfect texture of the milk.

Now you are ready to steam!
