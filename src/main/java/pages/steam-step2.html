2. Steaming the Milk

Now that you have your milk in your steam pitcher, brin git up to the steam pitcher. You should place the wand just below the surface of the milk.

You can now turn on the steam wand. If your milk is making a screeching noise, simply pull the wand out a bit and place it back in to let the air escape.

Once your milk reaches 120-140 fahrenheit, you can turn off the steam wand. Now you can prepare to pour!
